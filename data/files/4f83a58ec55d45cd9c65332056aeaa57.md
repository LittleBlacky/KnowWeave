# Company Access and Approval Policy

## Purpose

This demo policy describes how a fictional organization manages access requests,
expense approvals, security reviews, and evidence updates in KnowWeave. It is
safe sample content and does not describe a real company process.

## Approval Rules

- Access requests require manager approval before an account is provisioned.
- Finance reimbursement requests above 500 USD require a second approval from
  the budget owner.
- Security handbook exceptions require a documented risk review and an expiry
  date.
- Knowledge base changes must cite at least one policy paragraph or approved
  FAQ answer.

## Request Table

| Request type | Owner | Required evidence | Service target |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| System access | IT operations | Manager approval and ticket ID | 2 business days |
| Expense reimbursement | Finance | Receipt, project code, budget owner | 5 business days |
| Security exception | Security team | Risk review and expiry date | 3 business days |

## Evidence Governance

Every answer in the knowledge workspace should point back to a source file,
chunk, Knowledge Unit, or Wiki citation. If a citation is wrong, reviewers should
submit feedback and convert that feedback into an evaluation candidate.
